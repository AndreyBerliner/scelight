You can change the map image zooming by selecting the desired zoom value in the Zoom drop down list or you can hold down the [CTRL] key and use your scroll wheel (middle button) of your mouse.

The Map image is drag-scrollable. This means that if the map image does not fit on the screen and scrollbars appear, you can simply scroll the map image by dragging it with your mouse.

This is also indicated by the move mouse cursor.

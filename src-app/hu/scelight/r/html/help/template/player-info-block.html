The Player Info Block is a special symbol: it has a special opening and closing part ( < and > ) and some other symbols can be put inside of it.

When applying the name template on a replay, the template engine will evaluate and insert the contents of the Player Info Block for every player in the replay, grouping players by team and separating team members with a comma and teams with the " vs " string.

Example: In a 2v2 game the template:
<[PIBplayer] ([PIBrace])>
May result in:
Bob (T), Ben (Z) vs Rob (P), Ann (P)

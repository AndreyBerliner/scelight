The Template Engine supports sub-folders . This means that applying a name template on replays can categorize the replays into sub-folders.
Folders in the template are separated with the "[folderSep]" symbol.

Example: Let's say you want your replays in different folders based on the region they were played on, and inside the region folders have them categorized in folders based on game format (like 1v1, 2v2, FFA etc.).

The following template will do just that:
[region][folderSep][format][folderSep][dateTime] [map]
An example output:
Europe\1v1\2013-06-28 15_23_01 Star Station.SC2Replay

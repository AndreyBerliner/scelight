There are several ways to filter replays based on player properties using different Filter by Groups . These can be combined arbitrarily.

1. The Players Avg group contains properties which are aggregate values calculated from all the players of a replay (e.g. the weighted average APM of all players from a replay).

2. The Any Player group can be used to specify filters that may be matched by any player, independently from other player filters.

3. The All Players group can be used to specify filters that must be matched by all players, independently from other player filters.

4. The Player X groups (where X is the letter A, B, C or D) can be used to specify filters on different players and / or to specify multiple filters on the same player. The same X letter always refers to the same player, different X letters always refer to different players.

Example #1: Replays that have a player Dakota and have a player with more than 50 APM (which may or may not be Dakota):
[Any Player]Name='Dakota' AND [Any Player]APM>50
Example #2: Replays that have a player Dakota who has more than 50 APM:
[Player A]Name='Dakota' AND [Player A]APM>50
Example #3: Replays that have a player Dakota and have a different player with more than 50 APM:
[Player A]Name='Dakota' AND [Player B]APM>50
Example #4: Replays that have a player Dakota and have a different player with more than 50 APM and have a Protoss player (could be anyone):
[Player A]Name='Dakota' AND [Player B]APM>50 AND [Any Player]Race=Protoss
Example #5: Replays where the players have greather than 50 APM average and all players reached level 90:
[Players Avg]Average APM>50 AND [All Players]Levels≥90

Replay Filters are a list of conditions on replay properties, and they specify what replays to include in the Replay list table, excluding those that do not match the filters.

Filters can be connected with logical AND (both filters on its side must match) or with logical OR (only one filter on its side is enough to match).

The AND has higher precedence than OR , always the AND connections are evaluated first.
You can use different variations of the logical connection which insert parenthesis to group the filters . Filters inside parenthesis take precedence in evaluation. Missing opening parenthesis will be inserted before the first filter, and missing closing parenthesis will be inserted after the last filter.

Example #1: Replays that are from the Korean region with HotS expansion or are from the US region and were not played on the map Daybreak LE:
Region=Korea AND Expansion=HotS OR Region=US AND Map name≠'Daybreak LE'
Example #2: Replays that are not played on any Daybreak maps and are either from the Korean or US region:
Map name does not contain 'Daybreak' AND (Region=Korea OR Region=US)

The Multi-Replay Analyzer takes multiple replays as input, and calculates and displays aggregate statistics and development trends.

The Record V-D-O column displays the record of a player where V is the number of Victories, D is the number of Defeats and O is the number of Other results (e.g. Unknown or Tie).

Note: The average columns like Avg APM, Avg SPM, Avg SQ and Avg SC% are weighted averages where the weights are the length of plays the individual values are calculated with.

Note: The first letters of matchups in the Race Matchup and League Matchup columns always belong to the player whose details you are viewing.

Tip: Sorting a table by the Record column will perform a sort by:
V - D = (victories - defeats)
This logic takes the number of played games into consideration contrary to the Win % column. The result order can be used to decide which maps to veto down for example (the bottom of the list when sorted descending).
Example #1: A record of 4-36-0 (win rate = 11%) will be after a record of 1-10-0 (win rate = 10%) reflecting that a map of 4-36 record gives you more lost ladder points than a map with a record of 1-10.
Example #2: A record of 10-100-0 will be after a record of 1-10-0 even though having the same 10% win rate reflecting that 10-100 record would give you a lot more lost ladder points.

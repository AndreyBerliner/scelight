SPM stands for Screens Per Minute, it measures the multitasking skills of players.
SPM tells how fast / frequently players are changing screens during games. Numerically it shows how many times are screens switched by the player in 1 minute. Higher SPM means more / faster screen switches.

Screen changes are listed under the CameraUpdate events. Only screen changes that move the screen with at least a certain distance (delta ≥ 15) are counted in SPM .

That being said SPM includes:
  • Camera hotkey usage
  • (Left) clicking on the mini map to move your screen
  • Clicking on the portrait of the selected unit to center the unit on the screen
  • Pressing the space key to center the location of the latest event
SPM does NOT include:
  • Locking screen on a unit
  • Scrolling your screen with the cursor keys (keyboard)
  • Scrolling your screen by moving your mouse cursor to the side of the screen (side scrolling)
  • Spamming the same camera hotkey or (left) clicking on the minimap to approximately the same location
The SPM chart visualizes the current SPM value of players at each time of the game, averaging by the selected granularity time value.

Note: The static SPM of a player calculated for a game excludes the first 80 seconds (can be changed) and is only counted until the player's last command (Cmd event).

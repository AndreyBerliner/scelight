Charts can be zoomed in 2 ways which can be combined and repeated arbitrarily.

1. Zooming with markers

Markers are stored time values which are visualized on the charts as vertical dashed lines.

You can zoom any arbitrary time window of the charts by marking the beginning and the end of the time frame you want to zoom into, and pressing the Zoom into markers button.
You can set 2 markers by clicking on the chart with the left and right buttons of your mouse. The left marker is also synchronized to the events table : it is updated automatically to reflect the time of the selected event.

Note: The left marker if not set will be the beginning of the replay, the right marker if not set will be the end of the replay.

Tip: You can clear a marker if you hold down the [CTRL] key while clicking.

2. Zooming with mouse scroll wheel

Hold down the [CTRL] key and rotate your mouse scroll wheel up to zoom in or rotate down to zoom out . The mouse cursor will be the center of the zoom.

The Reset zoom button will always reset the zoom and show the whole charts.

Scrolling

When the charts are zoomed, a horizontal scroll bar appears under the charts which you can use to scroll the charts.

The charts are also drag-scrollable. This means that zoomed charts can simply be scrolled by dragging them with your mouse.

Tip: You can also enlarge the charts by clicking on the Enlarge charts button or clicking on the charts with the scroll wheel mouse button. Press the [ESC] button or click again with the scroll wheel mouse button to restore the enlarged charts.

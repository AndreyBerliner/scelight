The Favored Player List is a list of your favored players, typically your own accounts (including all if you have multiple accounts or if you play on multiple regions).

When you open or rename a replay, the Replay Processor rearranges players and their teams so that favored players (and their teams) are put ahead of others and therefore always displayed / listed first.

The Replay list table can also color rows based on match result of favored players.

Players are identified by a text referred to as the Toon which contains numbers that uniquely identify an account, realm and region.

Note: The player name is not part of the toon therefore if you use the Name change feature of Battle.net your toon remains the same. But arbitrary you can, and it's recommended to append the player name to the end of the toon separated with a dash character ( '-' ) so you will remember who that toon belongs to, for example:
2-S2-1-206154-DakotaFannin

The favored player list is specified by a comma separated toon list .

Tip: You can edit the favored player list manually or you can add / remove players from the Player menu by clicking on the Add to the Favored Player list or Remove from the Favored Player list items.

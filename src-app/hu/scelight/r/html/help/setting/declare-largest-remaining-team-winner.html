Many times in team games if a player leaves the game, the match result is not yet decided therefore the match result cannot (and will not) be recorded in the replay.

If the game result info is missing from the replay, ${appName} can deduce the winners by tracking the teams and leaving players, and declare the largest remaining team winner.

Since most of the times defeated players leave first, estimating winners by the largest remaining team gives a very accurate result.

Note: If the match result is recorded in the replay, it is never overridden.

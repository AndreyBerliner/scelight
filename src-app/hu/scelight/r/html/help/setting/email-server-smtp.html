${appName} can send emails in your name if you configure your email server and account. ${appName} does not provide you an email service, you have to own an email account which will be used directly.

Emails are sent by connecting and passing the email to the SMTP server of your email provider. Most email providers publish their SMTP server parameters in their help. You have to ask your email provider if you don't know or you can't find the SMTP server parameters in their help.
Your email provider may not grant you SMTP access in which case you will not be able to send emails via that email account.

Note: Your SMTP user password is NOT stored for security reasons, instead you will be asked to input your password before sending an email.

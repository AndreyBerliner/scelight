By default missing maps are downloaded from the region of the replay they are referenced from.
Many maps are also available on multiple regions to which you might have faster connection, and from which you might be able to download maps much faster.

You can enable to first attempt to download maps from your Preferred region , and only if the map is not available on this region will ${appName} revert back to the replay's region.

Tip: Select the region closest to your geographical location for faster map downloads.
